Know About the HALL

The hall was founded in 2011 and is the newest of all the 22 halls of IIT Kharagpur. The construction was finally completed in 2013.The five floored (G+4) building of the hall is divided into four blocks - A, B, C and D, each of them fully connected through AA, AB, BC, CD and DD blocks respectively. With an expandable capacity of more than 2000, it currently houses 1800 students.
